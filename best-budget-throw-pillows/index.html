Throw pillows are the easiest way to refresh a room without a full redecorating project, they add color, texture, and comfort for pocket change. But when you’re shopping on a budget, the options range from flimsy filler bags to genuinely well-made covers that last. The key is to focus on cover material (cotton, velvet, or woven synthetics), insert fill (polyester fiber is fine as long as it’s not lumpy), and closure type (zippers beat envelope flaps for adjustability).
