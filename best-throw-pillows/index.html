A good throw pillow is one of the easiest ways to refresh a room without a full redecorating budget. It adds color, texture, and comfort to sofas, beds, and accent chairs, but the wrong one can look flat or wear out quickly. When choosing, focus on fill material (down-alternative is easier to maintain, feather is softer), cover fabric (velvet for luxury, cotton for breathability, woven for durability), and size relative to your furniture.
